### Research Integrated

All location information was researched and verified:
- **Lijiang Old Town:** UNESCO World Heritage Site (1997), Naxi culture, Silk Road history
- **Jade Dragon Snow Mountain:** 4,680m elevation, 19 temperate glaciers, cable car experience
- **Impression Lijiang Show:** Zhang Yimou production with 500+ ethnic performers
- **Blue Moon Valley:** Copper ion glacial water creates turquoise color
- **Shangri-La/Zhongdian:** 3,300m altitude, 2001 renamed from "Lost Horizon" novel
- **Songzanlin Monastery:** Largest Tibetan monastery in Yunnan, built 1679, "Little Potala Palace"
- **Lamuyangcuo Lake:** Sacred "Holy Mother Soul Lake" with monastery reflections
- **Dukezong Ancient Town:** World's largest Tibetan prayer wheel (21-24m, 60 tons)

## 💡 Pro Tips for Gim & Girlfriend

1. **Altitude is Real:** Take Day 4-5 easy. Drink water constantly. Don't rush.
2. **Pack Smart:** Layers, sunscreen, warm hat, gloves. Temperature swings are 25°C between day/night.
3. **Photography Gold:** Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) at Jade Dragon and Lamuyangcuo is magical.
4. **Train to Shangri-La:** Book seats in advance. The scenery during the journey is stunning.
5. **Prayer Wheel Fun:** It takes multiple people to spin—make it a couples activity for good fortune! 🙏
6. **Food:** Try rice noodles (过桥米线) and yak dishes. Local flavors are incredible.
7. **Acclimatization:** You're jumping from sea level to 3,300-4,680m—respect the mountains.
